---

## About the School

Butemine School was established in **2013** by **John Nedura** and his spouse **Grace Ginduri**, who donated their family livestock to fund the school's founding. The name *"Butemine"* embodies the spirit of uniting the Sonjo/Batemi sub-tribes under one common purpose: fighting ignorance and ensuring no child is left behind.

The school serves marginalized children — including orphans — providing quality education while preserving indigenous Batemi culture, values, and traditions.

## Vision & Mission

**Mission:**
